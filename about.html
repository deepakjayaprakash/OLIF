About Us

Our outlook          

We operate on a business platform, with a global outlook and distant vision. In line with its borderless business approach, We strive to cross all the cultural barriers and promote universally recognized work practices. We believe that our business objectives and corporate mission can be sustained on a long term basis only by going beyond the brief and stepping outside conventional commercial wisdom.We strive to promote an infinite business relationship with our clients by trying to understand their underlying needs and provide customized services to exceed their expectations.Rather than equating everything in terms of cost, efficiency and time frames, we put our services on a mutually meaningful perspective which can lead to sustained growth. This proposition is driven by following attributes:

  • Pursuit of Excellence
  • You First
  • Commitment
  • Integrity & Accountability
  • Flexibility

Our Cuisine: Our Specialty

At our kitchen, we stand for good quality Indian food – fresh, wholesome food, prepared when you place an order. At our kitchen, we don’t use short cut.

Taste it. Remember us. Call again! Quality is our top priority Our commitment to quality extends to all parts of our business.

Your delicacies at your door step :

We use fresh vegetables. Our salads are prepared fresh when you order. We prepare food and use authenticated Indian masalas /ingredients based on customer requirements. We use in general less spicy food unless you ask for more spices.
